  • Miscellaneous Acronyms & abbreviations

    • ELEC -- Elective course at the introductory or nonintroductory level which does not meet a General Education Core requirement.
    • ELNI -- Nonintroductory level elective course which does not meet a general education core requirement.
    • ELUD -- Upper Division Elective.
    • NI -- Nonintroductory level course.
    • OTHA -- Arts and Sciences related course not offered at Mānoa.
    • OTHO -- Non-Arts and Sciences related course not offered at Mānoa.
    • SYLL -- Detailed syllabus needed.
    • UD -- Upper Division Level Course.
